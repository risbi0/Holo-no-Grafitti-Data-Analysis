Visualizations related to HoloGra appearances and episodes in general were made using MS excel. A list of episode appearances and debut episodes for each Holomem, appearance exclusions, and also a few interesting miscallaneaous stats, is posted [here](https://github.com/risbi0/Holo-no-Grafitti-Data-Analysis/blob/main/holomem_hologra.md).

Youtube comments were extracted using the Youtube Data API for the word cloud and its related charts, which were made using Python. An important thing to note is that latin character words were the only data that were analyzed.

Various methods were used in preprocessing the comments in order to clean it and reduce spam as much as possible. Such methods were: transforming to lowercase, only accepting words more than 2 characters long, removing links, mentions (though only the words right next to the @ sign, it doesn’t remove usernames separated by whitespaces), and duplicate words and comments, word tokenization, and lemmatization. From the 455k comments that were extracted, 183k comments containing 1.03m words remained after preprocessing.
 
Python was also used for generating the data for the appearance timelines (Fig. 3), and transforming the episode appearance list to a csv file, used by Fig. 1 & 2. And a little bit of Javascript was used for extracting the links in the playlist and for counting the total duration. If you're interested in seeing the source code, I published it in [github](https://github.com/risbi0/Holo-no-Grafitti-Data-Analysis).
